13th ACFEA Biennial Conference Announcement

2018-11-15

The Advisory Board has decided that the 13th ACFEA Biennial Conference(2020)
will be taken place at Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok, Thailand.
The host of this conference is Dr. Kua Wongboonsin, Professor and Interim Deputy Director of Sasin Graduate Institute of Business Administration, Chulalongkorn University.

on July 5-8, 2019 in Xiamen City, China.
All ACFEA members are welcomed and invited to attend.
