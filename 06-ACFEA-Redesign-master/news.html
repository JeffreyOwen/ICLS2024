(2020-06-15)

Official Announcement: After a long discussion of the organizing committee of 2020 ACES Conference, we have decided to host it as a virtual format and to cooperate with the 13th Biennial Conference of Asian Consumer and Family Economics Association (ACFEA) as a joint virtual conference. The date of this joint conference will be September 12 and 13, 2020 (Taipei time, GMT+8 hours)
